ABOUT US

We are The Company SheltMe

The SheltMe.ge website is a platform for newly arrived tourists in Georgia, where tourists will be able to request by publishing a post or searching for announcements made by citizens. It will be a shelter for sleeping only for a certain amount of money or even for free. citizens that must also be authorized on the website as a shelter giver, with the details of his shelter, for example: whether it is a private house or a building, he will also have to upload a photo of the environment that he offers the tourist for stay with him. After all this, the tourist can search for a suitable and satisfactory shelter for himself on the website, after which he can contact the number assigned by the citizen or write to him in the live chat on the website. In short, this platform is useful for those who have just arrived in Georgia and are still looking for a hotel but cannot find it because they do not have a budget for a hotel and urgently need only for overnight.

Our presentation is designed specifically for the Georgian people.
